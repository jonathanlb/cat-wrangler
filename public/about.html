Cat Wrangler RSVP Management

Click on subject headings to view instructions for how to use this webapp.

About

Cat Wrangler is an RSVP management system to collect group-member availability for event scheduling.

Your availability information is viewable only by the group's administrators. Other group members will only see aggregate availability, i.e. the total number of positive or negative responses for a time slot.

User Settings

Clicking on your name in the header will display the "User Settings" window with the following expandable options.

Personal

This section will list your name, a button to change your password, and a field listing your section.

You can change your section; just type in the new section and hit enter. You may only change to an existing section.

Blackout Dates

You may specify a list of dates that you cannot attend. Any date that you enter will RSVP negative for any existing and future event. You can override the negative RSVP manually in the "Events" tab.

Browse Events

You can view the active events under the "Browse Events" option. Click on an event name to expand the view.

You can express a preference to a time in the "Italian Flag" section -- the section comprised of rows of toggle switches, times, and a "heat bar" indicating the total number of positive and negative responses for the time, for example, in the static image below, you might see that you've clicked the left side of the switch, indicating a negative response, on the first row; the right side, indicating a positive response, for the second; and have not indicated availability for the third row. You can always reset your preference by clicking in the center of the switch.

Logout

The "Logout" button on the header will clear your login credentials and reset this webapp. Use this button when you're done if you share this computer, or just close the browser window.
